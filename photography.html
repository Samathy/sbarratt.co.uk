Samathy takes photos at events all over the country.

Works with Gay Pride Pics during the 'Pride' season travling the country and capturing LGBT+ Prides.

She is a freelance photographer who is open to working with any and all to photograph events.

You can see several examples of her work with GPP and other clients on her portfolio on Imgur


She also works with several other clients taking photos at different types of events.

Her previous clients include

  • Official photographer for InspireWIT 2017 @ Uni of Nottingham - Photos from the event here
  • The Silk Mill for several events including mutiple 'Silk Mill conversations' sessions and prototyping workshops. The Silk Mill Conversations Prototying Panel
  • The National Videogame Arcade for Gamecity Festival 2016 and The Oliver Twins book launch: Gamecity 2016, Oliver Twins


Samathy shoots with the high end Nikon D7100 DSLR and a good compliment of lenses. she prefers her prime lenses with her favorite being the 35mm.

Her earlier work was shot on a Sony A230 DSLR
