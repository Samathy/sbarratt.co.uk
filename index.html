Samathy Barratt is a passionate technologist and LGBTQ+ Activist.

Her primary interests lie in highly technical software engineering, new & cutting edge computer science practices, new software tools, languages and frameworks and cutting edge hardware.

She is a Coventry Pride Trustee and has been since 2015 helping to organise Coventry's LGBT Pride and surrounding events.

Samathy is also an event photographer, offering her services primarily to Gay Pride Pics But also working with other organisations.
